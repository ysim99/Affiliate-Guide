What are the requirements to join the affiliate program?

Individuals or businesses with a certain level of online traffic (visitor count) can apply for the Rinke Affiliate Program. However, applications will only be approved after a review to ensure they meet the eligibility criteria, and activities can only begin once approval is granted.


What is the duration of the contract?

The Ringke Affiliate Program does not have a fixed contract period. However, if there is no traffic or sales performance for a certain period, the program may be terminated without prior notice, so it is important to maintain consistent performance.


How is marketing material provided?

Ringke provides various promotional materials to assist partners with their marketing activities.


What is the commission structure?

The basic commission starts at 5%. Depending on performance and activity level, the commission rate may increase gradually.
